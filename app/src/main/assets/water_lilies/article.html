Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is a beautiful marble monument located in Agra, India. It was built by the Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in the 17th century as a tribute to his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal. This magnificent structure is often called a "poem in marble" due to its intricate and intricate architecture. It's not just a beautiful building; it's also a symbol of enduring love. The Taj Mahal attracts millions of visitors from around the world who come to admire its exquisite design and learn about the romantic history behind it. It's a must-visit landmark for anyone interested in history, architecture, or simply the magic of love captured in stone.
