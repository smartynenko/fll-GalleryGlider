Café Terrace at Night by Vincent van Gogh

This masterpiece have and many vibrant colors and many shades of colors. Vincent used blue and yellow in this painting he did. Van Gogh’s brushwork in this painting is just a sight itself. There are many patterns he narrated in his painting. One of the patterns in the swirling happening in the bright night sky. According to 1-start-gallery.com, “"Cafe Terrace at Night" is more than a visual representation of a scene; it is a portal into Van Gogh's inner thoughts and emotional landscape. The café itself takes on a symbolic role as a place of gathering, reflection, and solitude. It serves as a sanctuary where the individual can engage in introspection, far from the chaos of the world.” In Conclusion, this is avery great masterpiece to look at and get mesmerized into Vincent van Gogh’s own inner world.

This painting is located in the Kröller-Müller Museum in Otterlo, Netherlands.
